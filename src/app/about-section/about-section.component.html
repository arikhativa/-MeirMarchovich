About Us

Meir Marcovich Metals Ltd. was established on 1991, as a small commercial company which dealt in various scrap metals (solids and turnings), for recycling.

The company is giving gathering and pick-up services, as well as transportation all over the country. We are usually holding stock of various metals in our yard at South Industrial Zone Akko, as well as in additional yard at Kfar Bialik.

The company uses its own equipment such as tracks, cranes, baggers, fork-lifts, containers in various capacities, etc.
